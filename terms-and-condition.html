  • RIOSIS INNOVATIONS PRIVATE LIMITED does not accept cancellation requests for perishable items like flowers, eatables etc. However, refund/replacement can be made if the customer establishes that the quality of product delivered is not good.

  • In case of complaints regarding products that come with a warranty from manufacturers, please refer the issue to them.
  • In case of any Refunds approved by the RIOSIS INNOVATIONS PRIVATE LIMITED, it’ll take 6-8 days for the refund to be processed to the end customer.
